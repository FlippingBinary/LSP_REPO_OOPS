# Question 4 — AI Reflection

**Prompt 1:** Has your perspective on using AI in programming changed over the
semester? Briefly explain.

Over the course of the semester, I don't think my perspective on using AI in programming
has changed much. I had already begun using AI agents for certain tasks and found
them to be useful for boilerplate and repetitive tasks. Certain types of changes
are just significantly faster when an AI agent does it. Unfortunately, they can
be difficult to deal with at times, and I find that when I am doing something
truly novel I have to limit my AI usage to very specific tasks to reduce the risk
that it will go off the rails making something completely wrong.

**Prompt 2:** How do you plan to use AI in future programming or coursework?

I plan to continue using AI in future programming, but for coursework it depends
on the course. The technology is constantly evolving, and it obviously isn't going
away. Just like any tool, it has its uses.
